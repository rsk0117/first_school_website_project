Best choice of the Month

Japan - Cherry Blossom Festival

Don't miss out Japan's one of the exceptional seasonal events in the world! Japan is famous for its cherry blossom and the cherry blossom season is one of the most popular times to visit Japan. Viewing the blossoms is such a big event that national news services even carry maps of their progress, and it's a time when the Japanese throw away their reserve and decide to party. This tradition is to have a picnic party amid the blooming trees. You should join and enjoy this seasonal party!
